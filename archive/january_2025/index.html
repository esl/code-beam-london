THE ERLANG AND ELIXIR CONFERENCE
London | 31 January 2025

One day celebration of all things BEAM is coming to London!

This event offers a unique opportunity to meet locally, learn from industry experts, and network with fellow Erlang, Elixir and Gleam enthusiasts.

About the conference

This is a London event for Erlang, Elixir, Gleam and BEAM users

Our goal is simple: to create a vibrant platform where users of Elixir, Erlang, Gleam and other technologies based on BEAM VM can come together to share insights, get feedback on projects, and inspire each other to new heights.


Code BEAM has united Erlang & Elixir developers for years to grow and progress the community in the spirit of Share. Learn. Inspire. Meet people and companies using and developing high performance, fault-tolerant and resilient applications that scale to billions of users in production in areas like Fintech, eCommerce, IoT, Gaming, Blockchain, Security, Machine Learning and more.
